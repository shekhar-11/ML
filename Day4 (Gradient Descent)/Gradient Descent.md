Gradient descent is optimization algo in order to minimize the function (cost function)

it can be J(w1,w2,w3.....wn)
we need min of J(w1,w2,w3.....wn)


outline : take a random w,b in lin reg , 
keep changing w,b in such a way that cost function is decreasing 
until we reach the minimum point of cost function


Gradient descent is like you are at a hill and you want to reach the lowest point of the valley, you will keep going down the hill until you reach the lowest point.
so for that , take a 360 degree view of the hill and find the direction in which you will go down the hill, that is the nearest point with good slope, so you will keep going in that direction until you reach the lowest point of the valley.
